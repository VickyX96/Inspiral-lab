Frequently asked questions

// Things founders usually want to know before working with us

Why should we hire Inspiral Labs over other design Teams?

We're a founder-led creative studio built specifically for companies where design is a growth asset, not a commodity.

We don't just make things look good, we build brand systems and motion creative that convert communities, attract investors, and support launches. Our clients have used our work to secure ecosystem grants, raise capital, and grow token communities.
You're not hiring a generalist agency. You're hiring a studio that understands your market.

What industries do you work with?

We specialize in web3, DeFi, and AI companies.
These are high-stakes, fast-moving spaces where brand and motion quality directly impacts fundraising, community trust, and product adoption.

If you're building in one of these verticals, we understand your buyers, your investors, and your competitive landscape better than a generalist studio ever will.

What does your process look like?

Every engagement runs through Slack for day-to-day communication.

You'll have direct access to the team, no account managers, no middlemen. For new projects we align on scope and brief upfront, then move into delivery. Retainer clients have ongoing access with a 24–48 hour turnaround on standard requests.

How long does a project take

Our fastest turnaround for a new project is 5 days.
Larger engagements like full brand identities or launch packages run upto 2 weeks depending on scope. Timeline is always confirmed before work begins so there are no surprises.

What do we actually own after the project?

You own all final delivered assets in full; logos, motion files, social templates, landing page designs. Source files and working documents are transferred as part of a formal handoff, included in all packages.
Everything is yours to use, adapt, and build on.

Do you accept equity as compensation?

Case by case. If you're an early-stage company with strong fundamentals and a compelling product, we're open to discussing equity as part or full compensation.

We evaluate based on the stage of the company, the size of the equity offered, and how we see the project growing long term. Reach out and we'll have an honest conversation about it.

What if we already have a designer in-house?

Our clients often have internal designers.
What they don't have is a studio that has built brand systems that moved $6M in TVL and secured a $50k ecosystem grant.

We work alongside internal teams or as the lead creative, your internal designer handles execution, we handle the architecture they build from

Do you work with early-stage companies with limited budgets?

Our Launch Sprint at $3,500 is specifically designed for early-stage protocols and startups that need high-quality brand and launch creative without enterprise-level investment.

If budget is a genuine constraint, the equity conversation is also on the table for the right project.

What if we need work that doesn't fit one of your packages?

Custom scopes are available.
If your project is outside the standard packages, a one-off campaign, a specific launch asset, or a larger multi-phase engagement

Reach out with a brief via Email and we'll put together a custom proposal.
